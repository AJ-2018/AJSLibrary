Enormous Collection of Books

AJS Library has over 5 lakhs books in all subject areas such as Computer Science, Ethics, Law, Public Administration, Mathematics, Astronomy, Fine Arts, Travel etc. published by leading publishers across the world

Own Books Reading Sections

In ground floor of AJS Library there is a spacious books reading area where readers can bring their own books and with proper permission from the library authority.

Digital Library

Collections are stored in digital form and can be accessed through computers provided in the library. More than 3 lakhs e-Books and 40,000 e-Journals are available.
